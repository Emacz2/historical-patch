Civ Bonuses

**Champions**

Champion Hoplites are available in p1, and may promote to Hippeis. They attack 10% faster than other champion spearman. P1 take 20% less train but have 50 less health, -1 of hack and peirce resistance and do 15% less dmg.  It is gained back through phasing up

**Melee Infantry**

Melee Infantry 5% more dmg and gains an additional 5% p2 but Sparta does not have Will to Fight.

**Perioikoi Class**

Melee and Ranged 10% less dmg, Ranged, 10% less accurate. However, they gather resources 5% faster, have 5% faster walk speed, cost 10 less wood and train 10% faster

**Helots** 50 health, -20% dmg, 15% less accurate -5 range and +250 repeate time but train 15% faster and gather resources 25% faster and don't count torwards population

**Women**

Women 5% faster gathering/build rates rate, and 2% faster movement speed. Also 50% melee dmg and 0.5 of each resistance

**Warrior Culture**

-10% maximum population limit



**Team Bonuses**

Heroes train for free; Melee Infantry Heroes 10% more Pierce/Hack/Crush dm

Infantry +.5 hack, 1 pierce resistance and promote 15% faster.
